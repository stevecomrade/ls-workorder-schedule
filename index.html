Day-by-Day Calendar

See your workorder schedule for the next 7, 14, or 21 days. Expandable day rows show individual workorders with customer names and dates.

🚦

Traffic Light Badges

Green, yellow, and red count badges tell you at a glance which days are manageable and which are stacked. Thresholds are fully configurable.

💳

Store Credit Alerts

A banner appears automatically on any workorder where the customer has store credit on their account. Never miss a balance again.

📋

Line Counts & Totals

Expand a day to see how many line items and the dollar total on each workorder — no clicking into every WO individually.

🔒

Secure OAuth

Connects to Lightspeed via OAuth with credentials secured server-side. Your API secret never touches the browser.

GETTING STARTED

Up and running in 2 minutes

Install the extension

Grab it from the Firefox Add-ons store (Chrome coming soon). Click the extension icon in your toolbar to open settings.

Enter your Account ID

In Lightspeed, click Help in the bottom-left corner — your Account ID is shown in the panel that opens. Paste it into the extension popup.

Authorize with Lightspeed

Click "Authorize with Lightspeed" and approve the connection. The extension handles the rest — your schedule appears on any workorder page.

📅 Workorder Schedule

See your entire Lightspeed Retail workorder calendar at a glance — without leaving the workorder screen.

🦊 Firefox Add-on 🌐 Chrome Extension
📆

Day-by-Day Calendar

See all open workorders organized by due date across 7, 14, or 21 days — right inside Lightspeed.

🚦

Traffic Light Badges

Green, yellow, and red count badges show at a glance which days are light, moderate, or overloaded.

💳

Store Credit Alerts

A prominent banner appears on any workorder where the customer has a store credit balance on account.

🔧

Estimate Totals

Expand any day to see line counts and estimated totals for each workorder — no extra clicks needed.

🎨

Status Color Coding

Workorder cards show your actual Lightspeed status colors, automatically synced from your account.

⚙️

Configurable Filters

Choose which statuses to hide from the calendar — tailor it to your shop's workflow.

Setup in 3 Steps

1

Install the extension

Install from the Firefox Add-ons store or Chrome Web Store. No account needed — just click install.

2

Enter your Lightspeed Account ID

Click the extension icon and enter your Account ID. Find it in Lightspeed under Settings → API Access or in your browser's URL bar when logged in — it's the number after /Account/.

3

Authorize with Lightspeed

Click "Authorize with Lightspeed", approve the connection, copy the code from the page you land on, paste it back into the extension, and click Complete Setup. You're done.

FAQ

Is this free?

Yes, completely free to use.

Does it work with any Lightspeed R-Series account?

Yes — it works with any Lightspeed Retail R-Series account. It does not work with X-Series or other Lightspeed products.

Is my data secure?

All data is processed locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any third-party server. Your Lightspeed credentials are stored only in your browser's extension storage.

Where do I find my Account ID?

Log into Lightspeed and look at the URL — your Account ID is the number that appears after /Account/. It's typically a 5-6 digit number.

What browsers are supported?

Firefox and Chrome/Brave. Safari is not currently supported.
